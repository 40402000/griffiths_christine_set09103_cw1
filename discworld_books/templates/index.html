Welcome to the Discworld Books App!

This site gives you every possible reading order for Terry Pratchett's Discworld series of books. With a massive 41 books in the Discworld series, it can be difficult to know where to begin, or even what's next... Find your perfect reading list below!

Chronologically

Have time on your hands? Or you're a stickler for order? The definitive list: all the Discworld books, ordered by the year of publishing, this is the reading list for you.

The City Watch

Policing Ankh-Morpork has never been easy, will the addition of dwarves, trolls and vampires make it any easier? Sam Vimes leads The City Watch whose motto is FABRICATI DIEM, PVNC.

The Witches

When shall we three meet again? In the next book in the series. Granny Weatherwax, Nanny Ogg and Magrat Garlic star in The Witches series, alongside faries, ghosts and various anthropomorphic personifications.

Death

Death isn't so bad, think of it as leaving the party early to avoid the rush. Oh and he likes curry and cats, "CATS, CATS ARE NICE." NON TIMETIS MESSOR.

Moist Von Lipwig

Forced into modernising Ankh-Morpork with institutions such as the Post Office and the Clacks, Moist has been known to participate in extreme sneezing to make life more exciting.

Rincewind/The Wizards

Rincewind isn't a particularly successful alumni of Unseen University. However, he is a successful survivor of many life-threatening adventures, employing his suprisingly effective technique of "running away". NVNC ID VIDES, NVNC NE VIDES.

For Younger Readers

Terry Pratchett's Discworld books for younger readers include the Carnegie Medal award-winning "The Amazing Maurice and his Educated Rodents. With an incredible cast of characters including Feegles, witches and talking rats.
